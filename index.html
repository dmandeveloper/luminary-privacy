Luminary Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-05-28  |  Last Updated: 2026-05-28

1. Overview

Luminary ("the App") is developed by Deeman Gaming ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

The short version: Luminary does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you. The App contains no user accounts, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising systems. Your gameplay data is stored only on your device.

2. What Data Does Luminary Collect?

Luminary itself collects no data. All gameplay progress, settings, and level selections are stored locally on your Android device. We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

What About Google Play Store?

Google Play Store may collect standard app distribution metrics (installs, uninstalls, crashes) as part of the Play Store platform. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, not by Luminary. Luminary does not control or access any of this data.

No Third-Party Tracking SDKs

Luminary contains no analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or telemetry SDKs. The app bundles only:

  • Google Play Licensing (com.google.android.vending.licensing): Required by Google Play to verify purchase authenticity. Does not collect or transmit user data.
  • Standard Android/Kotlin libraries (AOSP, Guava): Core system utilities with no data collection.

The "No ads. No trackers. Just puzzles." claim is fully accurate.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • User accounts or login credentials — Luminary requires no account
  • Analytics or usage data — We do not track how you play or which levels you solve
  • Advertising or demographic data — No ads, no ad networks, no profiling
  • Crash reports or error logs — No crash reporting SDK
  • Location data — Luminary does not request or use your location
  • Device contacts, photos, or files — Luminary does not request these permissions

4. Data You Control

Your puzzle progress (levels completed, settings, saved data) is stored only on your device. You can delete it by:

  1. Clearing the app's data: Settings → Apps → Luminary → Storage → Clear Data, or
  2. Uninstalling the app (which will also remove all saved progress)

5. Permissions & Why Luminary Requests Them

Luminary declares zero Android permissions in its manifest. The app requires no INTERNET, no location, no device ID, no contacts, camera, or file access.

If your device reports permissions when installing from Play Store, those are system-level permissions from Android OS or Play Store itself, not requested by Luminary.

6. Data Retention

Since Luminary does not collect personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete. Gameplay data on your device is yours to manage.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

Luminary is designed for a general audience, including children. Luminary:

  • Does not collect personal information from children or anyone else
  • Does not target marketing at children
  • Does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or under the age of digital consent in your jurisdiction)

If you believe a child's personal data has been collected by Luminary, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights & Data Subject Requests

Because Luminary does not collect or store personal data, there is no data to access, correct, delete, or port. If you have questions about your privacy, please contact us directly.

GDPR (EU Users)

If you are in the EU, GDPR applies to your use of the Android platform and Google Play Services, but not to Luminary directly (since we collect no data). For inquiries related to Google's data practices, see Google's Privacy Policy.

CCPA (California Users)

If you are in California, CCPA grants you rights to know, delete, and opt-out of the "sale" of personal information. Luminary does not collect, sell, or share personal information, so CCPA rights do not apply to Luminary. Your rights apply to Google Play Services' practices.

9. Data Security

All gameplay data is stored locally on your device and is subject to your device's security settings. We do not transmit gameplay data to any server.

10. Third-Party Links

The About screen may contain links to external websites (e.g., social media, developer websites). This Privacy Policy does not apply to those sites. Please review their privacy policies independently.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. If we make material changes (e.g., we add analytics or change how we handle data), we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date and posting the new policy here.

Your continued use of Luminary after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

If you reside in California, CCPA may apply to data practices. For questions about your local privacy rights, consult a local attorney or your privacy regulator.

This policy is effective as of the date it is published and applies to all versions of Luminary on Google Play Store.
